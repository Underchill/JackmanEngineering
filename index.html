The Solution

Engineered for the Unexpected.

Traditional toilet installation requires the floor and pipe to be perfectly aligned. They rarely are. The Jonny Joint utilizes a patented telescopic male/female flange system that adapts to the environment, not the other way around.

  • Universal Fit: Compatible with 3" and 4" waste lines.
  • Zero-Wax Seal: High-strength sealant eliminates mess and biological degradation.
  • Secure Mounting: Hollow bolt clamping plates ensure rigid connection to the subfloor.
  • Retrofit Ready: Perfect for renovations where floor height changes.
