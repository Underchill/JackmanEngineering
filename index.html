How JONNYJOINT Works

A Revolutionary Dual-Stage System.

The JONNYJOINT system replaces the unreliable wax ring with a precision-engineered, two-part four bolt coupling that creates a mechanical, high-performance seal. The process converts a standard ceramic toilet into a modern "slip fitting" through the following technical stages:

1. Toilet-to-Flange Conversion

The upper male section is installed with the toilet inverted. A new ASTM-approved adhesive sealant is applied to the face of the male flange.

Verified Seal: Unlike "blind" wax seals, the plumber can witness the adhesive squeezing out around the entire 7" circumference, ensuring a 100% leak free bond.

2. Subfloor Integration

The lower female floor flange provides a four bolt system: two anchor bolts and two septic bolts.

  • Direct-to-Floor Attachment Metal plates are embedded into the PVC flange and screwed directly into the subfloor.
  • Stress Transfer Anchoring into the floor structure transfers stress away from the DWV piping.

3. The Final O-Ring Connection

Once both sections are prepared, the final connection is completed without heavy lifting.

  • Slide-In Installation The toilet is slid into place, dropping the male extension into the female flange.
  • Dual-Stage Nitrile Butadiene Rubber Seal A high-quality Nitrile Butadiene Rubber O-ring creates a 100% leakproof and gas-proof seal.
