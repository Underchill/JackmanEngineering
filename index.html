How JONNYJOINT Works

A Revolutionary Dual-Stage System.

The JONNYJOINT system replaces the primitive wax ring with a precision-engineered, two-part coupling that creates a mechanical, high-performance seal. The process converts a standard ceramic toilet into a modern "street fitting" through the following technical stages:

  • Universal Fit: Compatible with 3" and 4" waste lines.
  • Zero-Wax Seal: High-strength sealant eliminates mess and biological degradation.
  • Secure Mounting: Hollow bolt clamping plates ensure rigid connection to the subfloor.
  • Retrofit Ready: Perfect for new builds and remodeling.
